Rims Collection

This pack includes 10 highly detailed 3d models , of both oem, aftermarket and generic rim designs. All of the models can be subdivided for close up shots or be left unsubdivided for reduction of memory usage. All of the rims are uv unwrapped and some rims include removable decals. The pack also includes a procedural material that can add scratches and edge wear to all the models using ambient occlusion and edge detection (can be used in blender cycles) the color , saturation , amount of scratches , the intensity of the scratches and the roughness of the material can be adjusted non destructively and procedurally through the easy to use material node.

The rims that are included in the pack are:
  • Stern face ii
  • Work Meister s1 3p
  • Fifteen52 Turbomac
  • Oz rally racing (2000s design)
  • BBS lm
  • Audi quattro OEM rims
  • BMW Z4 gt4 OEM rims
  • Ferrari F40 OEM rims
  • 6 spoke rims
  • 3 spoke multi piece rims

Disclaimer: some rims require at least one level of subdivision to be shaded properly The materials only work in the .blend format in the cycles render engine.
